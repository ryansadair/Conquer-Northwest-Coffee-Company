Brew Guide

Best Ways to
Brew Deep Forest.

Recommended

French Press

1:12 ratio · 4 min steep · 200°F

The ideal method for Deep Forest. Full immersion brewing preserves the rich oils and smoky body that make this dark roast sing.

AeroPress

1:10 ratio · 2 min · 195°F

Fast, concentrated, and smooth. Great for camping or the trail — use the AeroPress Go and a JetBoil for a proper backcountry brew.

Drip

1:15 ratio · Standard cycle

Works well for an everyday morning pot. Use filtered water and a burr grinder for consistent results every time.
