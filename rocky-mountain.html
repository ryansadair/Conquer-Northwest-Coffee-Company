Brew Guide

Best Ways to
Brew Rocky Mountain.

Recommended

Pour Over

1:16 ratio · 200°F · 3–4 min

The medium roast shines in a pour over — clean, bright, and balanced. Bring out the caramel sweetness with a slow, controlled pour.

Recommended

Drip

1:15 ratio · Standard cycle

The most versatile blend for everyday drip brewing. Consistent and approachable — the perfect pot for a full house or a slow morning.

AeroPress

1:12 ratio · 195°F · 2 min

A great trail option. The medium profile works well concentrated — smooth and satisfying without the bitterness of a darker roast.
