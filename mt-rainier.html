A Mountain That Demands Respect

Rising dramatically from the surrounding lowlands, Rainier is visible from much of Washington. Its massive glaciers and wildflower-filled meadows in July and August make it one of the most rewarding destinations in the Pacific Northwest.

Whether you're day hiking from Paradise, backpacking the Wonderland Trail, or attempting the summit, Rainier rewards those who come prepared.
