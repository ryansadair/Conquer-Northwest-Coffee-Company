Brew Guide

Best Ways to
Brew the I5.

Built For This

Espresso

1:2 ratio · 9 bar · 25–30 sec

The I5 was dialed in specifically for espresso. Pull it tight — a 1:2 ratio in 27–30 seconds delivers the best balance of crema and body.

Moka Pot

Medium-fine grind · Medium heat

No espresso machine? The moka pot is the next best thing. The I5 makes a rich, stovetop concentrate that holds up beautifully with milk.

Drip

1:15 ratio · Coarser grind

Works great in a standard drip machine — use a coarser grind than you would for espresso. Rich, dark, and satisfying for a morning pot.
